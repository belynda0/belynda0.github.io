PROJECTS

Hidden Homicide Capstone

My research focuses on understanding where cases become cold or stall—whether that’s at the level of law enforcement, the DA, the medical examiner, the judiciary, or due to policies that may cause delays. I aim to develop a machine learning model that can predict and cluster cases, potentially revealing hidden patterns and helping us formalize practices that prevent cases from moving forward (e.g., lack of restraining orders, high-risk lifestyles, etc.) and classify the type of case .
