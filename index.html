Alliance for Hope International

At Alliance for Hope International, we are dedicated to breaking the cycle of violence and providing comprehensive support for families affected by trauma. Our mission is to create safer communities by focusing on healing, prevention, and creating justice-driven solutions for survivors. We collaborate with a diverse network of partners to promote systemic change, enhance victim advocacy, and ensure that those affected by violence have access to the resources and support they need.

As a Team Member, I am involved in the Hidden Homicides Project, where I conduct an in-depth review of cold case files to analyze, classify, and extract critical data. I work on building a machine learning algorithm to categorize cases as murder, suicide, or hidden homicide, offering valuable insights to guide case assessments.

Additionally, I engage in policy reviews to deepen our understanding of legal implications and investigative frameworks, ensuring that our research is grounded in the most current and relevant legal standards. By providing consulting insights, I help streamline project goals and ensure thorough, evidence-based case assessments. I work with sensitive and confidential information to support the development of comprehensive research findings that contribute to finding justice for victims.

I am proud to be part of this transformative project and to contribute to the ongoing efforts to enhance advocacy for vulnerable communities.
